# Crow: Your Local AI Life Architect

**Crow** is a hyper-personalized, offline-first assistant designed to bridge the gap between "having a goal" and "actually finishing it." Unlike cloud-based AI that provides generic advice without context, Crow lives on your device, tracks your daily execution, and self-corrects your path based on real-world feedback.

##What Makes Crow Different?

Most AI tools are passive. Crow is **active**. It doesn't just give you information; it manages your journey.

* **Closed-Loop Accountability:** Crow doesn't just set a task; it waits for your feedback. It analyzes how you performed and adjusts tomorrow’s workload accordingly.
* **Source-Based Course Generation:** Provide a source (PDF, Link, or Topic), and Crow generates a complete, structured learning journey—breaking it down into daily, manageable milestones.
* **The "Daily Audit" System:** Every day, Crow generates a personalized checklist. At the end of the day, it checks in to see how you maintained your goals and where you struggled.
* **Self-Correction Logic:** If you fall behind or lose focus, Crow doesn't just "reset." It asks targeted questions to find the root cause and recalibrates your journey to get you back on track.
* **Deep Contextual Memory:** Because it runs locally, Crow remembers every win, every failure, and every habit—building a "Perfect Notice" of your growth over time.

## Core Capabilities

| Feature | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Dynamic Checklists** | Generates high-priority tasks every morning based on your long-term goals. |
| **Growth Tracking** | Visualizes how you are maintaining your goals and doing things "properly." |
| **Personalized Feedback** | Analyzes your daily input to give advice that is specific to your struggles, not general AI fluff. |
| **Educational Architect** | Turns raw data/sources into a full curriculum with tasks to accomplish the goal. |

## Privacy & Performance
* **100% Local:** Your goals, failures, and personal data never leave your machine.
* **Zero Latency:** No "waiting for server" responses—your coach is always ready, even offline.
* **No "API Memory Loss":** Cloud models "forget" after a few chats. Crow uses local persistent storage to keep your entire history alive.
